About Westward – What Even Is This Game?

Howdy, partner. Welcome to Westward, a multiplayer open-world game set during the wild and gritty days of U.S. western expansion (1803–1861).

What’s the game actually about?

  • Build stuff – Form towns, build communities, or start your own tribe.
  • Explore huge maps – Inspired by Yellowstone, Utah, and the American West.
  • Choose your side – Settlers or Tribal Leaders.
  • Make your own story – Be peaceful, aggressive, creative... it's your journey.
  • Mess around with friends – Or strangers. It's your call.

What’s the Cyfrex thing?

  • DLC faction – cold-adapted lizard race with honor and feathers.
  • Special tactics: hit-and-run, spiritual beliefs.
  • You'll meet them... eventually.

Why this server?

  • We're building Westward now. Volunteers needed!
  • Writers, artists, devs, lore nerds – anyone cool welcome.
  • Feedback is gold. Questions are good. Excitement is everything.
